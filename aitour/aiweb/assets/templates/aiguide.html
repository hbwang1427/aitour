Convenience

Listen to what you see.

Stories behind each art deserves a careful listening. With conventional audio guide, visiter has to search and type a unique number assigned to each artwork. AiGuide lets you listen directly to what you see.

Mobile phone should be your visit engagement rather than distraction. This is the core value of AiGuide.

Technology behind the app

Enabled by most advanced image recognition technology

Deep convolutional neural networks enables accurate image recognition against challenging image angles and qualities.

Image recognition is fully employed on your mobile. In this way, personal information including the images captured will not leave out of your phone at all.

The default 'photo' mode supports immediate prediction to just one photo capture. It may predict incorrectly when the captured photo is inproper.

The 'Live' mode predicts only when the art in view could be correctly recognized.

Copyright

AiGuide only includes the MET artwork in the public domain.

On February 7, 2017, The Metropolitan Museum of Art implemented a new policy known as Open Access, which makes images of artworks it believes to be in the public domain widely and freely available for unrestricted use, and at no cost, in accordance with the Creative Commons Zero (CC0) designation and the Terms and Conditions of this website.

It also makes available data from the entire online collection―both works it believes to be in the public domain and those under copyright or other restrictions―including basic information such as title, artist, date, medium, and dimensions. This data is available to all in accordance with the Creative Commons Zero (CC0) designation.

How to use

  • 1
    Launch the APP

    For first-time use, image matching models will be automatically downloaded. Access to your camera will be asked as well. If agreed, click on 'tour' and camera is ready to capture your interests.

  • 2
    Snap your interests

    All you need do now is pointing your phone camera to your interested artwork and snapping a photo. The higher the photo quality is, the more accurately the image match will perform.

  • 3
    Enjoy the stories

    After seconds of calculating, photo will be matched and audio stories will be returned. As long as the art is under CC0 and in our database, the top returned will be right with 95%. If not, scroll down to check the rest of the returned.
